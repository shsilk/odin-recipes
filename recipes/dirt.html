Vanilla Dirt Dessert with gummy worms

Vanilla Dirt Dessert is a delicious treat made with pudding and Oreos. It is a fun, sugary option for birthdays and other celebrations.

This recipe is from an old family friend back in the 1900s. It has withstood the test of time as a wonderful and tasty family tradition.

Ingredients

  • 16oz (at least) of crushed Oreos
  • 8oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 pkgs vanilla pudding (big pkgs)
  • 1/2 c. conf sugar
  • 3 c. milk
  • 12 oz cool whip

Instructions

  1. Cream butter, sugar, cream cheese
  2. Add pudding, milk, and vanilla on low speed (BEWARE OF SPLASH)
  3. Fold in cool whip by hand
  4. Line pot w/ some oreos
  5. Layer-alternate filling with oreos
  6. Final layer should be oreos
  7. Chill in fridge at least 6 hrs.
  8. Put a flower in pot! Serve!
